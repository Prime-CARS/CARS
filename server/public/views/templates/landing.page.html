+ What​ ​is​ ​the​ ​C.A.R.S​. ​ministry? - What​ ​is​ ​the​ ​C.A.R.S​. ​ministry?

The C.A.R.S​. acronym stands for Christian Auto Repair Shop. We are a part of the nonprofit ministry, Youth Impact Racing (Y.I.R). Y.I.R is a gospel-centered, Christian program where youth learn about racing through RC cars and Go Karts while building relationships and developing life skills. Older teens have the opportunity to work on full-size cars with mentors from the C.A.R.S​. Ministry. Mentors range from experienced mechanics to people who enjoy auto repair, maintaining cars, and the camaraderie of other car repair enthusiasts. Regardless of skill level, all mentors value providing service for people in need.

+ Who​ ​does​ ​C.A.R.S​. ​serve? - Who​ ​does​ ​C.A.R.S​. ​serve?

C.A.R.S​. serves single moms, single women, widows, financially limited individuals, and others who may need assistance with vehicle repair as our schedule permits.

+ Be​ ​Involved​ ​with​ ​C.A.R.S​. ​Ministry! - Be​ ​Involved​ ​with​ ​C.A.R.S​. ​Ministry!

Volunteers are always needed to help with car repair and food preparation. Training is provided. If you want to use your time and talents to serve others, please contact Mitch at 612-382-6828 or email cars@youthimpactracing.org for more information. Please consider supporting the Christian Auto Repair Shop ministry with a tax deductible donation. Donations go towards covering the cost of garage supplies, tools, online manuals, insurance, and automotive parts for individuals in need. Please mail donations to: C.A.R.S​. 1613 Southcross Drive West Burnsville, MN 55306

+ How​ ​much​ ​do​ ​repairs​ ​cost? - How​ ​much​ ​do​ ​repairs​ ​cost?

All labor for vehicle repair is provided free-of-charge by volunteer mechanics and mentors. Individuals eligible for vehicle repairs pay for the cost of parts required for the service or repair. C.A.R.S​. obtains needed parts from various local auto part suppliers at a substantial discount for our guests.

+ C.A.R.S​. ​ ​Hours​ ​of​ ​Operation​ ​and​ ​Location - C.A.R.S​. ​ ​Hours​ ​of​ ​Operation​ ​and​ ​Location

All appointments must be scheduled. No drop-ins are accepted. The shop is open the first Saturday of each month, unless it conflicts with a major holiday weekend. The garage is located at: 20685 Lynn Drive Prior Lake, MN 55372.

+ What type of repairs can we do?
-What type of repairs can we do?
  • Oil and oil filter change
  • Check Engine light diagnosis
  • Brake repair including:
  • Disc Pads, Shoes, Rotors, Drums, Calipers
  • Minor tune ups including:
  • Spark Plugs, Wires, Coil Replacement
  • Replacement of:
  • Serpentine belts
  • Battery
  • Headlights
  • Tail and brake lights
  • Air Filter
  • Windshield Wiper Blades
  • Struts and Shocks
  • Tie rods
  • Stabilizer bar links
  • Some drive shafts
  • Wheel bearings
-What don't we do?
  • Timing Belts
  • Water pumps
  • Transmission repair or flushes
  • Exhaust repair
  • Major engine repair
  • Body repair
** All cars must be in driving condition. We do not offer service calls or towing service. **
